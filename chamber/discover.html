Wild Life Safari Park

456 Safari Park Blvd, Crescent City

A unique park experience featuring walking trails through natural habitats, exotic animals, and guided safari tours for families and nature enthusiasts.

Downtown Farmers Market

789 Market St, Crescent City

Enjoy fresh produce, local crafts, and live music every weekend at the heart of the city.

Artisan Coffee House

654 Brew Lane, Crescent City

A cozy spot to enjoy locally roasted coffee and pastries, featuring works by local artists.

Greenwood Garden

987 Flora Rd, Crescent City

Stroll through beautifully curated gardens showcasing native and exotic plant species.

Seaside Art Walk

412 Coastal Blvd, Crescent City

Explore outdoor murals and sculptures from local artists along this scenic coastal path.

Harborview Skate Park

202 Grind St, Crescent City

A popular spot for skaters and bikers, featuring ramps, rails, and graffiti art.

Historic Train Station

123 Heritage Ave, Crescent City

Step back in time at this beautifully restored train station showcasing vintage locomotives and artifacts, a favorite spot for history buffs and families alike.

Folk Dance Pavilion

77 Festival Road, Crescent City

Enjoy lively performances of traditional dances and music in this vibrant pavilion that celebrates the city’s cultural heritage.
