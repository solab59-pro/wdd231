This site name represents the name of the avaition school that focuses on educating and provided professional courses relating to the aviation sector

SOLAB SCHOOL OF AVIATION SITE PURPOSE

1. Provide several courses for student to choose from and then become enrolled to be trained

2. Offer job and internship opportunities to students on completion of the course training

3. Bring to view to the general public our partners and partnership deals that makes us a great choice to consider when choosing an aviation school

4. Offer options for student to choose off camp or on camp accommodation

SCENARIO OF THE SITE

The Scenario seeks to answer the following question

1. What are the available courses provided by the school for enrolment

2. What are the cost of each course and what to consider before choosing a course
